What is the Care Home Broker platform?

The platform is a private deal portal for UK care home businesses. Sellers can quietly indicate their interest in a sale, and vetted buyers can review opportunities, send enquiries, and receive curated matches – all with a focus on confidentiality and quality, not volume.

It sits between “nothing happening” and “full agency process”: structured enough to move a deal forward, light-touch enough to protect day-to-day operations and staff.

How it works

1. Create your profile

Sellers outline their care home(s) and appetite to sell. Buyers set preferences for region, care type, size, and price band.

2. Listings & matching

Sellers create confidential listings. Buyers see relevant opportunities in their dashboard, not a noisy public marketplace.

3. Structured enquiries

Buyers send enquiries behind an NDA gate. Sellers receive, track and manage responses without sharing sensitive details too early.
