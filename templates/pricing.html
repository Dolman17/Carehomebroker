Frequently asked questions

How is confidentiality handled?

Listings are kept off public marketplaces. Buyers only see headline, non-identifying information until NDAs are in place and the broker agrees it’s appropriate to share more detail.

Do I have to upgrade to Tier 2 to get value?

Not necessarily. Tier 1 is useful if you simply want to be on the radar and let the broker contact you when something clearly fits. Tier 2 is for organisations that want to actively browse, compare and engage.

How does the broker get paid on a successful deal?

Alongside any subscription, an agreed success fee or percentage of the transaction value is payable if an introduction from the platform leads to a completed deal. This is agreed directly with Kaijo North Consulting.

Can valuers join without taking on full marketplace access?

Yes. Many valuers start on Tier 1 to get listed in the framework, then move to Tier 2 if they want deeper data visibility or closer collaboration on deal flow.
