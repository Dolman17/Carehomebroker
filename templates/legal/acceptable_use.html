Automated or unusually heavy use that affects service performance may be limited. Access levels are personal to the subscribed account and may not be resold or used to build a competing database.

Report suspected misuse to {{ legal_contact_email }}. We may investigate, preserve relevant records, remove content, restrict access or suspend an account. Where appropriate and lawful, we may notify affected users, service providers, regulators or law-enforcement bodies.
